RAM

Registrar Appointment Manager (RAM) is a user-friendly mobile application designed to streamline the process of scheduling appointments with the Registrar's Office. With RAM, you can effortlessly book, reschedule, or cancel appointments, ensuring a hassle-free experience for both students and staff.

Undertale

Introducing our innovative project designed for individuals seeking a quick and accessible solution for painting, drawing, or creating various lines and forms without the need for physical markers or whiteboards. Our research has identified a significant challenge, especially among students, who may find it difficult to afford a new mini-whiteboard. Our project serves as an excellent tool for crafting captivating images, beautiful illustrations, drawings, or text/letter writing. This free application is user-friendly, making it accessible to everyone, ensuring that creativity knows no bounds.
